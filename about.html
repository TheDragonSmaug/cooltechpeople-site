About Cool Tech People

A curated network of the live events industry's most trusted, experienced, and talented technical professionals — built to connect excellence with opportunity.

Our Mission
Excellence, Made Visible

The live events industry runs on talent. Behind every spectacular show, every flawless broadcast, every immersive experience is a team of deeply skilled technical professionals — people who know their gear inside and out, who've solved impossible problems under pressure, and who've quietly made the impossible look easy.

Cool Tech People exists to make that talent visible. To create a network where the best in the business are recognized, discoverable, and accessible to the companies seeking exactly that level of reliability and excellence.

How It Started
Five Years In The Making

Cool Tech People grew out of something simple: a private Slack channel of about forty live events professionals who trusted each other. Over five years, that channel became an invaluable resource — a place to troubleshoot, share leads, find collaborators, and recommend the right person for the right job.

The quality of that group was undeniable. These were media server operators, video directors, projection designers, broadcast engineers, and technical directors who had collectively worked on some of the most demanding productions in the world. Not because they were loud about it — but because the work spoke for itself.

The question was simple: why keep that hidden? Cool Tech People is the answer — a public-facing home for a community that was already thriving in private.
